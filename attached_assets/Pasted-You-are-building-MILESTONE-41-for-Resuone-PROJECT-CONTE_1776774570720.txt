You are building MILESTONE 41 for Resuone.

PROJECT CONTEXT
Resuone already has or is implementing:
- global job discovery
- CV parsing
- CV vs JD analysis
- tailored CV generation
- cover letter generation
- application tracker
- notifications
- billing + plans
- recruiter workspaces
- candidate profiles
- interview prep
- internal and external job matching

NEW PRODUCT GOAL
Build a FULL INTERNAL HIRING MARKETPLACE inside Resuone where:
1. recruiters can post jobs directly on Resuone
2. Pro users (and above) can see Pro-only posted jobs
3. users receive notifications for relevant posted jobs
4. users can analyze their fit before applying
5. users can apply manually or with assisted/automated flow
6. users can track application progress
7. recruiters can manage applicants through a hiring pipeline
8. recruiters can message candidates directly inside Resuone
9. recruiters can invite candidates to interviews
10. candidates can receive, review, and respond to interview invites
11. internal jobs are integrated into the broader discovery and matching engine

This milestone should make Resuone feel like a real two-sided hiring platform.
